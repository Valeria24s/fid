A delicious olive spread perfect for appetizers.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black olives
  • 2 tbsp capers
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients in a food processor.
  2. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  3. Serve with crackers or bread.
