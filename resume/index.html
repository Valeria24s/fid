About Me

Hi, I’m Valeria Silva, a passionate digital artist currently studying at Seminole State College. My love for animation and storytelling started when I first watched Avatar, inspiring me to pursue a career in the animation industry. I’m working towards a future at Disney Studios, where I hope to bring characters to life and create immersive worlds.

Right now, I’m developing my skills in 2D/3D animation, character design, and digital illustration. In addition to my studies, I volunteer at a help center assisting kids learning English, which has strengthened my communication and leadership skills. My goal is to push the boundaries of animation and tell stories that inspire others the same way they inspired me.
